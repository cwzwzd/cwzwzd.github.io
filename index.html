Racial Equality In Hongkong

Rationale for Interest

Racial equality refers to a situation in which people of all races are treated firly(Cambridge dictionary,2024).Racial equality is the key point of protecting human rights,”everyone is entitled to all the rights and freedoms set forth in this Declaration, without distinction of any kind, such as race” (Universal Declaration of Human Rights,1948).We need to fight against the ideologies or behaviours that against racial equality like racism, for it deepens mistrust, casting suspicion on all sides and tearing apart the social fabric, harms a society of all(United Nations,2024).

Problems of Racial Equality

According to the report released by relative human rights organisation, the main problems the ethnical minorities are facing in Hong Kong is the language barrier and discrimination(TREATS HK,2021).Our research will be mainly focused on this 2 parts of problems
  • Discrimination
      • Workplace Discrimination
      Daily Life Discrimination
  • Language Problem
      • Communication Barriers
      Educational Challenges
      Access to Public Services

    Proposed Plan of Action for Advocating for Changes

    Regarding the issue of discrimination, the Hong Kong SAR Government is committed to eliminating racial discrimination and promoting the development of ethnic minorities Equal opportunity. To address language barriers, both the government and the private sector have proposed and taken some measures.
